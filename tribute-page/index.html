P. Ramlee

Malaysian Actor, Singer, Director, Musician and Songwriter

Here's a time line of P. Ramlee's life...

1929

  • Born in Penang on March 22nd, 1929 to Teuku Nyak Puteh Teuku Karim and Che Mah Hussein

1945

  • '3rd Place' in North Malaya Singing Contest, Radio Malaya (Penang)

1946

  • Performed with 'Orkes Mutiara'
  • Performed with 'Pancharagam Rangkaian Melati'
  • Performed with 'Sinaran Bintang Sore'
  • '2nd Place' in North Malaya Singing Contest, Radio Malaya (Penang)
  • '1st Place' performing with 'Pemuda Indonesia Keronchong Party' in Penang Keronchong Singing Contest (May 1946)

1947

  • Performed with 'Coronation Amateur Party'
  • Performed with 'Teruna Sekampong'
  • Adopted the name 'P Ramlee'
  • '1st Place' in Radio Malaya Singing Contest (Penang)

1948

  • Performed with 'Pancaragam Mustika'
  • Film director B S Rajhans from Malay Film Productions spotted P Ramlee during a performance at the Malay Agriculture Show in Butterworth (1 Jun 1948)
  • Moved to Singapore on Idil Fitri to join Malay Film Productions (8 Aug 1948)
  • Film actor in 'Chinta' / 'Love' (Released: 31 Oct 1948)

1949

  • Performed with 'Kembang Murni Keronchong Party'
  • Film actor in 'Noor Asmara' (Rlsd: 13 Apr), 'Nasib' / 'Fate' (Rlsd: 25 Jul), 'Nilam' (Rlsd: 28 Sep)

1950

  • Married Junaidah Daeng Harris
  • Film actor in 'Rachun Dunia' (Rlsd: 16 Feb), 'Bakti' (Rlsd: 3 Apr), 'Takdir Illahi' (Rlsd: 19 Sep), 'Aloha' (Rlsd: Dec)

1951

  • Film actor in 'Penghidupan' (Rlsd: 23 May), 'Sejoli' (Rlsd: 19 Jun), 'Juwita' (Rlsd: 12 Oct)
  • 'Bintang Pujaan Malaya-Indonesia' / 'Favorite Artiste Malaya-Indonesia' - P Ramlee by Film Raya Magazine

1952

  • Film actor in 'Antara Senyum Dan Tangis' / 'Between Smile And Tears' (Rlsd: 27 Feb), 'Anjoran Nasib' (Rlsd: 19 Apr), 'Patah Hati' / 'Broken Heart' (Rlsd: 2 Aug), 'Sedarah' (Rlsd: 30 Aug), 'Miskin' / 'Poor' (Rlsd: 30 Nov)
  • 'Filem Terbaik' / 'Best Film' - 'Antara Senyum Dan Tangis' / 'Between Smile And Tears' by Film Raya Magazine
  • 'Pelakon Terbaik' / 'Best Actor' - P Ramlee by Film Raya Magazine
  • 'Filem Pujaan' / 'Favorite Film' - 'Juwita' by Film Raya Magazine

1953

  • Won Radio Malaya's 'Bintang Radio Lelaki' / 'Best Male Singer'
  • Birth of first son Mohamad Nasir (Mother was Junaidah)
  • Film actor in 'Putus Harapan' / 'Lost Hope' (Rlsd: 2 Mac), 'Ibu' / 'Mother' (Rlsd: 13 Jun), 'Hujan Panas' (Rlsd: 15 Aug), 'Siapa Salah?' / 'Who is Guilty?' (Rlsd: 7 Nov)
  • The first issue of 'Bintang' magazine produced by P Ramlee and friends (Rlsd: 10 Mac 1953)
  • 'Filem Terbaik' / 'Best Film' - 'Hujan Panas' by Film Raya Magazine
  • 'Pelakon Terbaik' / 'Best Actor' - P Ramlee by Film Raya Magazine
  • 'Filem Pujaan Malaya' / 'Favorite Film Malaya' - 'Antara Senyum Dan Tangis' / 'Between Smile And Tears' by Film Raya Magazine
  • 'Filem Pujaan Malaya - Indonesia' / 'Favorite Film Malaya-Indonesia' - 'Antara Senyum Dan Tangis' / 'Between Smile And Tears' by Film Raya Magazine
  • 'Bintang Pujaan Malaya - Indonesia' / 'Favorite Artiste Malaya-Indonesia' - P Ramlee by Film Raya Magazine

1954

  • Birth of second son Arfan (Mother was Junaidah) *
  • Film actor in 'Panggilan Pulau' (Rlsd: 4 Jul), in 'Perjodohan' (Rlsd: 24 Jul), 'Merana' / 'Suffer' (Rlsd: 27 Nov)
  • 'Filem Terbaik' / 'Best Film' - 'Hujan Panas' by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine
  • 'Seniman Terbaik' / 'Best Male Artiste' - P Ramlee by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine
  • 'Seniwati Terbaik' / 'Best Female Artiste' - Siput Sarawak ('Hujan Panas') by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine
  • 'Seniman Pembantu Terbaik' / 'Best Supporting Male Artiste' - Haji Mahadi ('Hujan Panas') by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine

1955

  • Divorced Junaidah Daeng Harris
  • Producer in radio drama 'Jiwa Putera Melayu' (1 Jan 1955)
  • Married Noorizan Mohd Noor (6 Feb 1955)
  • Film actor in 'Abu Hassan Penchuri' / 'Abu Hassan The Thief' (Released: 2 May 1955)
  • Film director/actor in 'Penarek Becha' / 'Trishaw Man' (Released: 30 Oct 1955)
  • 'Filem Terbaik' / 'Best Film' - 'Penarek Becha' / 'Trishaw Man' by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine
  • 'Pengarah Terbaik' / 'Best Director' - P Ramlee ('Penarek Becha' / 'Trishaw Man') by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine
  • 'Seniman Terbaik' / 'Best Male Artiste' - P Ramlee by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine
  • 'Lagu Terbaik' / 'Best Song' - 'Inang Baru' ('Penarek Becha' / 'Trishaw Man') by Utusan Film and Sport Magazine

1956

  • Film actor in 'Hang Tuah' / 'Legend Of Hang Tuah' (Rlsd: 28 Jan), 'Anak-ku Sazali' / 'My Son Sazali' (Released: 27 Oct)
  • Won 'Best Music' for 'Hang Tuah' / 'Legend of Hang Tuah' at 3rd Asian Film Festival, Hong Kong (May 1956)
  • Director in stageplay 'Jiwa Putera Melayu' (Jun 1956)
  • Film director/actor in 'Semerah Padi' (Released: 14 Jul 1956)
  • 'Filem Terbaik' / 'Best Film' - 'Semerah Padi' by Bintang Magazine
  • 'Filem Terbaik Kedua' / 'Second Best Film' - 'Anak-ku Sazali' / 'My Son Sazali' by Bintang Magazine
  • Pengarah Terbaik' / 'Best Director' - P Ramlee ('Semerah Padi') by Bintang Magazine
  • 'Pengubah Lagu Terbaik' / 'Best Composer' - P Ramlee by Bintang Magazine
  • 'Biduan Terbaik' / 'Best Singer' - P Ramlee by Bintang Magazine
  • 'Seniman Terbaik' / 'Best Male Artiste' - P Ramlee by Bintang Magazine

1957

  • 'Hang Tuah' shown at International Film Festival, Berlin
  • Won 'Best Male Lead Role' for 'Anak-ku Sazali' / 'My Son Sazali' at 4th Asian Film Festival, Tokyo (May 1957)
  • Film director in 'Panca Delima' (Released: 7 Jul 1957)
  • Film director/actor in 'Bujang Lapok' / 'Three Bachelors' (Released: 30 Nov 1957)

1958

  • First issue of 'Gelanggang Filem' entertainment tabloid produced by 'Penerbitan P Ramlee' (Released: 15 Mac 1958)
  • Film director/actor in 'Sumpah Orang Minyak' / 'Curse Of The Oily Man' (Released: 20 Apr 1958)
  • Won 'Best Black and White Photography' (Abu Bakar Ali) for 'Sumpah Orang Minyak' / 'Curse of the Oily Man' at 5th Asian Film Festival, Manila (May 1958)
  • Additional film director/actor in 'Sergeant Hassan' (Released: 28 Aug 1958)
  • Birth of third son Sazali (27 Dec 1958)
  • 'Filem Terbaik' / 'Best Film' - 'Sumpah Orang Minyak' / 'Curse of the Oily Man' by Kisah Bintang Dan Filem Magazine
  • 'Seniman Lelaki Terbaik' / 'Best Male Artiste' - P Ramlee by Kisah Bintang Dan Filem Magazine
  • 'Lagu Terbaik Kedua' / 'Second Best Song' - 'Tudong Periok' ('Sumpah Orang Minyak' / 'Curse of the Oily Man') by Kisah Bintang Dan Filem Magazine
  • Conferred the rank of 'Sergeant Titular' by the Malay Army Regiment in appreciation of P Ramlee's role in 'Sergeant Hassan' that boosted the morale of its ranks.

1959

  • Film director/actor in 'Pendekar Bujang Lapok' / 'The Three Warriors' (Released: 21 Mac 1959)
  • Won 'Best Comedy' ('Pendekar Bujang Lapok' / 'The Three Warriors') at 6th Asian Film Festival, Kuala Lumpur (May 1959)
  • Film director/actor in 'Musang Berjanggut' (Released: 1 Aug 1959)
  • Writer/director in stageplay 'Sultan Mahmood Mangkat Di Julang' (Sep 1959)
  • Film director/actor in 'Nujum Pa' Blalang' / 'Fortune Teller' (Released: 26 Dec 1959)

1960

  • Performed with 'Pancha Sitara'
  • Won 'Best Comedy' ('Nujum Pa' Blalang' / 'Fortune Teller') at 7th Asian Film Festival, Tokyo (May 1960)
  • Film director/actor in 'Antara Dua Darjat' (Released: 28 May 1960)
  • Performed with 'P Ramlee Keronchong Orkes' (Oct 1960)
  • Performed with 'Pancha Sitara II' (Dec 1960)
  • 'Filem Sukaramai' / 'Favorite Film' - 'Pendekar Bujang Lapok' / 'The Three Warriors' by Gelanggang Magazine
  • 'Teraju Sukaramai' / 'Favorite Director' - P Ramlee by Gelanggang Magazine
  • 'Seniman Utama' / 'Top Artiste' - P Ramlee by Gelanggang Magazine
  • 'Lagu Sukaramai' / 'Favorite Song' - 'Jangan Adek Angan Angan' ('Musang Berjanggut') by Gelanggang Magazine

1961

  • Divorced Noorizan Mohd Noor
  • Film director/actor in 'Ali Baba Bujang Lapok' / 'Ali Baba In Burlesque' (Released: 31 Jan 1961)
  • Writer/director in radio stageplay 'Kachep Mas' (3 Jun 1961)
  • Film director/actor in 'Seniman Bujang Lapok' / 'The Nitwit Movie Stars' (Released: 6 Jul 1961)
  • Married Salmah Ismail a.k.a. Saloma (21 Nov 1961)

1962

  • Director in stageplay 'Damaq'
  • Film director/actor in 'Ibu Mertua Ku' / 'My Mother In-Law' (Released: 7 Mac 1962)
  • Film director/actor in 'Labu Dan Labi' / 'Labu And Labi' (Released: 29 Aug 1962)
  • Conferred the 'Ahli Mangku Negara (AMN)' by The King of Malaysia HM Tuanku Syed Putra Jamallulai (27 Sep 1962)
  • Nominated for 'Best Comedy' - 'Seniman Bujang Lapok' / 'The Nitwit Movie Stars' at 9th Asian Film Festival, Seoul

1963

  • Guest star in 'Love Parade' (Released: Jan 1963)
  • Film director/actor in 'Nasib Si Labu Labi' / 'Labu And Labi 2' (Released: 20 Apr 1963)
  • Won 'Most Versatile Talent' and 'Best Black and White Photography' (Abu Bakar Ali) for 'Ibu Mertua Ku' / 'My Mother In-Law' at 10th Asian Film Festival, Tokyo (May 1963)
  • Won 'Best Comedy' - 'Love Parade' at 10th Asian Film Festival, Tokyo (May 1963)

1964

  • Film director/actor in 'Madu Tiga' / 'Rival Three ' (Released: 12 Feb 1964)
  • Film director/actor in 'Tiga Abdul' / 'Rival Three ' (Released: 22 Apr 1964)
  • Won 'Best Comedy' ('Madu Tiga' / 'Three Rivals') at 11th Asian Film Festival, Taipeh (May 1964)
  • Guest star in 'Melanchong Ke Tokyo' (Released: Oct 1964)
  • Moved to Kuala Lumpur to join Merdeka Film Productions (16 Apr 1964)
  • Film director/actor in 'Sitora Harimau Jadian' / 'The Tigerman' (Released: Nov 1964)

1965

1971

1972

  • Actor in TV drama (3 episodes) 'Rantau Selamat'
  • Film director/actor in 'Laxmana Do Re Mi' / 'Do Re Mi 3' (Released: 1972)
  • Contract with Shaw Bros terminated
  • Formed 'Sazara Film' with Sanusi Junid, Zainuddin Nordin and Abdullah Hussain

1973

  • Formed 'Rumpun Melayu Film' with H M Shah (Feb 1973)
  • Attended 19th Asian Film Festival in Singapore (18 May 1973)
  • Sudden death of heart attack at 5.30 a.m. (29 May 1973)
